# Materials

##What You Will Need

* 4 Raspberry Pis
* 4 Micro-USB cable (the cable you would use to charge an Android phone)
* 4 microSD cards with adapters
* 1 HDMI cable
* 4 Ethernet cable
* 1 USB keyboard 
* 1 USB Mouse
* 1 4-port ethernet switch
* 1 4-port usb hub
